Living with alopecia or hair thinning doesn't mean living without confidence. This program is about expert styling, scalp care, and protective approaches designed specifically for Black and Brown people with textured hair.

Black and Brown people with alopecia deserve a stylist who sees them, understands their hair, and knows how to make them feel like themselves again.

Why This Program Exists

Styling with
intention & care

Most stylists aren't trained to work with alopecia clients thoughtfully. Styles get placed without considering the impact on thinning areas. Tension is applied where there should be none. And clients leave feeling more exposed than when they walked in.

That's not what happens here.

The Crown Restoration Program is a specialist-led styling and scalp care approach for clients managing alopecia, thinning edges, or hair loss. Nay is not a medical provider and does not diagnose or treat alopecia — but with 20+ years of expertise in textured hair, she knows how to work with your hair where it is right now, and how to make you feel confident and beautiful in the process.

If you're dealing with hair loss, you deserve a stylist who handles your crown with knowledge, gentleness, and genuine care — not someone who pretends the thinning isn't there.

"You deserve a stylist who works with your hair where it is — not one who ignores what's happening with your crown."

Client result — Crown Restoration Program
Is This Program For You?

This program is for you if…

🪮

You're experiencing thinning or alopecia

You want a stylist who takes your hair loss seriously and approaches your crown with care — not someone who applies styles without considering the impact on thinning areas.

💆🏾

Your scalp needs gentle, informed attention

Dryness, sensitivity, or tenderness that makes salon visits stressful. We work carefully and deliberately, always checking in about what feels right for your scalp.

🌱

You want styles that work with your hair, not against it

Protective styles designed to minimize tension on fragile areas, cover thinning spots beautifully, and let you leave the salon feeling like yourself — confident and cared for.

✂️

You've been mishandled by other stylists

Styles too tight, tension applied to already fragile edges, no acknowledgment of what's happening with your hair. You need a reset with someone who puts your hair's wellbeing first.

🧭

You're living abroad and don't know where to turn

Hard water, humidity, unfamiliar products, and no local stylist who understands your hair or your situation. The expat experience is real — and it's accounted for here.

👩🏾‍🦱

You want locs but have thinning edges

Locs may still be an option — but it requires an honest, careful assessment first. We never recommend a loc journey without understanding the full picture of your hair health.

The Program

How the Crown
Restoration Program works

This is a structured, personalized approach — not a single appointment. Each step is designed to meet your hair where it is, work with it carefully, and build toward consistent, confidence-restoring results. All Crown Restoration clients begin with a consultation before any service is scheduled. Nay is not a medical provider and does not diagnose or treat alopecia — but she brings 20+ years of specialist expertise to every client's care.

01
Assessment
Understanding
your crown

We begin with a thorough hair and scalp consultation. No assumptions, no rushing — just an honest conversation about your hair, your history, and what approach makes the most sense for you.

  • Hair & scalp condition review
  • Thinning area assessment
  • Lifestyle & product history
  • Custom styling plan development
02
Scalp Restoration
Reset the
foundation

A clean, calm scalp is the starting point for everything. We provide gentle scalp care to address buildup, dryness, and sensitivity — creating the best possible environment for your existing hair.

  • Gentle scalp cleanse & treatment
  • Botanical oil application
  • Tension-free handling throughout
  • Scalp-supportive product recommendations
03
Confidence Styling
Look and feel
like yourself

This is where your crown gets its confidence back. We create protective styles that beautifully work with thinning areas — covering what you want covered, celebrating what you have, and minimizing stress on fragile hair.

  • Alopecia-aware protective styling
  • Low-tension installation throughout
  • Style options tailored to your thinning areas
  • At-home maintenance guidance
04
Maintenance
Keep the
crown thriving

Consistent, ongoing care is what sustains your results. We build a maintenance rhythm that keeps your scalp healthy, your style fresh, and your confidence intact — appointment after appointment.

  • Ongoing scalp care check-ins
  • Membership pathway available
  • Mérida climate adjustments
  • Long-term hair care strategy
