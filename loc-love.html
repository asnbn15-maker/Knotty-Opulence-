Environment Matters

What Mérida
does to your locs

Most loc care advice was written for temperate climates with different water. Mérida changes the equation entirely. Understanding your environment isn't optional — it's the foundation of every good decision you'll make for your hair while you're here.

💧

Hard Water Buildup

Mérida's water is high in minerals — calcium and magnesium that deposit on the hair shaft and scalp over time. In locs, this buildup is invisible until it causes brittleness, dullness, and thinning. A regular Loc Detox is not a luxury here. It's maintenance.

🌡️

Humidity & Moisture Balance

High humidity doesn't mean your locs are hydrated. The moisture in the air competes with — and often disrupts — the moisture balance your locs need. Over-moisturized roots and dry ends are common. Your product routine must account for this.

☀️

UV Exposure

The Yucatán sun is intense year-round. UV exposure breaks down the protein structure of the hair shaft and causes color to fade faster — even natural melanin. Proper sealing and the right products create a barrier your locs need in this climate.

self-care.
Protect it.

Living abroad is beautiful and it is hard. It's easy — especially in the early months, or in the slow seasons — to let the things that take care of you slip. The appointments that feel optional. The routine that feels like too much effort.

Living in a new country, navigating daily life in a different language, far from your community — it takes a toll. It's easy to slip into isolation, to let the things that ground you become "someday" habits. Your hair care is one of those things that grounds you. It is a practice of showing up for yourself in a consistent, intentional way.

Sitting in Nay's chair — getting your locs tended, your scalp assessed, your questions answered — is an act of grounding. This is your community. Your specialist. Your place to exhale.

A Note on Timing

Plan your day around your appointment.

Appointments here are intentional and unhurried. Depending on your hair's condition, the service, and what comes up during your visit, your appointment may run longer than estimated. This is not an inconvenience — it is the nature of specialist care done right. Please do not schedule other commitments immediately before or after. A rushed client creates a rushed appointment, and rushed appointments produce results that neither of us will be happy with. Give your hair — and yourself — the time it deserves.
