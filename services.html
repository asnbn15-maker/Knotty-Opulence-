The Microloc Journey

Microlocs —
A Full Day's Work

Microlocs are one of the most time-intensive services offered at Knotty Opulence. The install alone is a full-day commitment — the precision of each individual part, the hundreds of sections, the technique — it cannot be rushed. A Hair & Lifestyle Consultation is required before booking. No exceptions.

Once installed, microlocs need consistent, skilled maintenance. The retightening method, scalp health, the integrity of each loc — these are not services to hand off. A package or membership means your microlocs stay in the hands of the specialist who installed them.

Why It Works

The case for committing

01

Income secured in advance

A client with a package or membership is a client whose money is already in the bank. That predictability lets me plan, prepare, and show up fully for every appointment.

02

Your spot is actually protected

I keep an intentionally limited client list. Package and membership clients get priority booking — when my schedule fills, you're not scrambling. Your appointment is already there.

03

Consistency makes locs thrive

Skipping maintenance, going too long between appointments, switching stylists — these are the things that slow progress and cause damage. Committing to a schedule is committing to your locs.
